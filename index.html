About Me

My name is Crystal, the proud owner of Azul Lotus Boutique. As a high school music teacher for over 17 years, I continuously strive to encourage my students through affirmations and ambient music. Being an educator has taught me to be profoundly grateful to work with exciting young minds and help steer them toward the directions in life where they yearn to flourish. After years of teaching and motivating our youth, I have realized that my deep-seeded passion resides in positive thinking and self empowerment. I love discovering both unique Vintage & Antique jewelry pieces and also creating hand-made accessories.

Seventeen years and many pieces of jewelry later, I am ready to unveil to the world the Azul Lotus Boutique. When you purchase an item from Azul Lotus Boutique, you will gain access to my website, admittance to my beneficial affirmations, and a 10-minute relaxation ambient music session. I have found that people use affirmations to boost their self esteem and outlook on life. My jewelry is perfect for a night out on the town, an intimate low key day, having cocktails with friends or a gala affair. The vintage, retro and contemporary pieces can be found for both Men and Women. Check out my jewelry and find your wondrous treasure today.
